Let The Magic Shine At DisneyLand

DisneyLand Paris 30th Anniversary

The Disneyland Paris 30th Anniversary celebrations will be more magic and epic than ever as we come to the end of this once-in-a-lifetime celebration... Enjoy the festivities until 30 September 2023!

Become Part Of the Marvel Universe

Attention recruits! Prepare to assemble for Marvel Avengers Campus in Walt Disney Studios Park. Discover epic attractions, experiences and your favourite Marvel Super Heroes!

Make the Magic Last around the Clock

Discover the benefits of spending the night at Disneyland Paris! With a Disney Hotel+Ticket package, immerse yourself in the magic just a short walk or shuttle ride away, greet Disney Characters at your hotel and enjoy the Parks before they officially open.​

The Disneyland Paris experience

Escape to the heart of the Disney, Pixar, MARVEL and Star Wars universes that you love so much. Disneyland Paris is full of activities for all ages and all tastes!

Don’t miss this magical Disney Parade!

Magic is in the air as the world-famous Disney Stars on Parade passes through Disneyland Park! Admire Disney Characters on their colourful floats against a backdrop of enchanting music and immerse yourself in Disney stories like never before.

Embark on a live musical journey with Pixar pals

Don't miss this original, immersive new production at Walt Disney Studios Park that will take you on a journey through the wonderful worlds of Toy Story, Monsters, Inc., Finding Nemo and many others. Enjoy 30 minutes (approx.) of live music, innovative choreography and theatrics, and appearances from many beloved Pixar characters! Please note: this show will take a scheduled break on 6 November 2023 and will come back in December 2023.​

Disney Symphony of Colors

We’re remixing the winter blues with something colourful, musical and… magical! It’s called Disney Symphony of Colors, and it’s coming to Disneyland Park in early 2024. Full details will be revealed soon... But until then, here's a sneak peek of the vibrant new experiences that will be sure to shake up some of the greyest months of the year and bring a smile to your face! From 8 January, gaze in amazement as a new drone show illuminates the night skies above Disneyland Park with a palette of brilliant colours*. Then, from February, enjoy a new daytime show bursting with beloved songs and stories starring Disney and Pixar Characters*.​
